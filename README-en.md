### 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm a 3rd-year **Systems Engineering** student at UTN (GPA: 7.88 | 17 subjects passed). I am passionate about stepping out of theory and writing code that solves real-world problems.

* 🏢 **Real-world experience:** I develop and maintain "Antigravity", a comprehensive web-based management and billing system for hardware stores. It is built with React and Firebase, focusing on performance optimization and ensuring an agile, scalable deployment for SMEs.
* 🤖 **AI-Powered Development:** My workflow integrates advanced tools. I handle multiple AIs simultaneously and am certified in the **2026 AI Development Course by Big School**. I use *Prompt Engineering* to design clean architectures and accelerate development.
